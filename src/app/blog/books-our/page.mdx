## Nepali Book Recommandation

### 1. "समयको सिपाही" (Samayko Sipahi) by Amar Neupane

- Genre: Fiction, Psychological Thriller
- Description: "समयको सिपाही" (Samayko Sipahi) follows the story of a man named Anil who finds himself trapped in a time loop, reliving the same day over and over again. As Anil struggles to unravel the mystery behind his predicament, the novel explores themes of fate, free will, and the nature of reality.

### 2. "सेतो धरती" (Seto Dharti) by Amar Neupane

- Genre: Fiction, Social Commentary
- Description: "सेतो धरती" (Seto Dharti) depicts the struggles of a Nepali village during the Maoist insurgency. The novel follows the lives of various characters as they navigate the turbulent political landscape and grapple with questions of loyalty, morality, and survival.

### 3. "कर्णाली ब्लुज" (Karnali Blues) by Buddhisagar

- Genre: Fiction, Coming-of-Age
- Description: Through the eyes of its protagonist, Karnali Blues offers a poignant coming-of-age tale set in the remote Karnali region of Nepal. The novel explores themes of family, tradition, and personal growth as the protagonist embarks on a journey of self-discovery amidst the rugged beauty of the Himalayas.

### 4. "साया" (Saya) by Subin Bhattarai

- Genre: Fiction, Romance
- Description: "साया" (Saya) follows the story of Aarav and Sirish, two individuals whose lives intersect in unexpected ways. As they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and longing, the novel captures the essence of modern Nepali romance with its poignant narrative and relatable characters.

### 5. "समर लभ" (Summer Love) by Subin Bhattarai

- Genre: Fiction, Romance
- Description: "समर लभ" (Summer Love) is a heartwarming tale of love, friendship, and self-discovery set against the backdrop of college life in Kathmandu. Through the intertwined destinies of its characters, the novel explores themes of youthful passion, dreams, and the bittersweet journey of growing up.

### 6. "इजोरिया" (Ijoriya) by Subin Bhattarai

- Genre: Fiction, Drama
- Description: "इजोरिया" (Ijoriya) delves into the lives of its characters, each grappling with their own inner demons and desires. As their paths converge and diverge, the novel offers a poignant exploration of human nature, relationships, and the search for meaning in a world fraught with uncertainties.

### 7. "पुष्पाली" (Pushpali) by Parijat

- Genre: Fiction, Romance
- Description: "पुष्पाली" (Pushpali) is a timeless love story set in the picturesque hills of Nepal. Through the protagonist Pushpali's journey, the novel explores themes of love, sacrifice, and societal expectations, offering a poignant portrayal of the human heart's capacity for both joy and sorrow.
